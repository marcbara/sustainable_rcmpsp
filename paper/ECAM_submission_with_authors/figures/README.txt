FIGURES NEEDED — IJCM Submission
================================

Place the following 4 files in this folder before compiling:

  1. pareto_front_mild.png      — Fig 1(a): Pareto front, mild scenario
  2. pareto_front_moderate.png  — Fig 1(b): Pareto front, moderate scenario
  3. pareto_front_severe.png    — Fig 1(c): Pareto front, severe scenario
  4. convergence_mild.png       — Fig 2:    Convergence curves, mild scenario

DPI requirements (IJCM / Taylor & Francis):
  - Line art:   1200 dpi
  - Grayscale:   600 dpi
  - Colour:      300 dpi

Accepted formats: TIFF, EPS, PS, JPEG
Regenerate with:  plt.savefig("filename.png", dpi=1200, bbox_inches="tight")

NOTE: For submission, figures are also uploaded as separate files
      in the Taylor & Francis portal (not only inside the ZIP).
